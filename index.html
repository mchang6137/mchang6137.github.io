Introduction

Hi! My name is Michael Alan Chang and I am a first year Ph.D student in the CS department at UC Berkeley, where I currently work with Scott Shenker. I am broadly interested in networks, distributed systems, and smart cities. Previously, I was an undergraduate at Princeton University, where I worked on SDN, network resiliency, and analytics with Jennifer Rexford, Laurent Vanbever, and Theo Benson. I am supported by a NSF GRFP Fellowship
